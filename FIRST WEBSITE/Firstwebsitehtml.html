About Dungeons and Dragons

Dungeons and Dragons (DND 5e) is a Tabletop RPG, a game in which people roleplay as characters in a world narated by a Dungeon Master, and every action they make is determined success by the roll of a dice.

Making a character

In order to make a character, one has to do a few things. First, choose a Race, What species you wish to play. Then choose a class, what do you wish to be good and and what abilities do you have. And thirdly, make a backstory as a way to understand how to play your character. Not that this is a very simple version of making a character, and that this is for show purpose.
